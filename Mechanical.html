Fix a Bike Chain that Falls off

  1. Put part of the chain back onto the back cog.
  2. Slowly reattach the chain onto the bottom teeth of the rear cog.
  3. After that, attatch the chain into the bottom of the front chain.
  4. Once it is mostly attatched to the teeth, push the pedal forward until the chain fully connects.
  5. If the chain keeps on falling off after reconnecting it, you may need to shorten the chain.
